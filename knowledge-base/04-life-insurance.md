# Life Insurance — Coverage Details

ShieldBase **term life** products pay a death benefit to named beneficiaries if the insured dies during the term, subject to policy terms and timely premium payment.

## What is typically included

- **Level term** — Death benefit stays fixed for the chosen term (e.g., 10, 20, or 30 years).
- **Accelerated death benefit rider (if elected)** — May allow access to part of the benefit for documented terminal illness, per rider terms.
- **Conversion option (if offered)** — May allow switching to a permanent product before a stated deadline, with new premiums based on age and health class at conversion.

## Common exclusions

Typical exclusions in many term policies include (exact language is in the contract):

- Death within contestability period if material misrepresentation is found
- Suicide within the first one or two policy years (state-specific)
- Death resulting from illegal activity or war (as defined in the policy)
- Misstatement of age or gender affecting premium or benefit

Life insurance does **not** build cash value in standard ShieldBase term products unless you convert to a permanent form that includes that feature.

## Underwriting

Issuance depends on application answers, possibly a medical exam, and prescription/MIB-style checks where applicable. Not all applicants qualify for all preferred classes.
