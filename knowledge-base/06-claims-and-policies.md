# Claims Process and Policy Rules

## Filing a claim

1. **Safety first** — Move to a safe location; call emergency services if there are injuries or hazards.
2. **Report promptly** — Use the app, portal, or 24/7 claims line. For auto, exchange info and document the scene with photos when safe.
3. **Get a claim number** — Save it for all follow-ups.
4. **Cooperate** — Provide statements, photos, receipts, and access for inspection when requested.
5. **Settlement** — Review the estimate and payment explanation; ask questions before accepting if anything is unclear.

## Deadlines (illustrative)

- **Auto / property:** Notify ShieldBase **as soon as practical**, and generally within **72 hours** of discovering damage for many losses, or **30 days** for theft—**your policy’s notice clause controls**.
- **Proof of loss:** If we send a formal proof-of-loss form, return it by the date stated (often **60 days** from request unless extended).
- **Life:** Beneficiary should begin the process **within a few weeks** of death; full payout timing depends on review and required documents (death certificate, claim forms).

## Deductibles

- **Auto collision/comprehensive** — You pay the deductible per covered claim before we pay the remainder (unless waived in a covered not-at-fault scenario where state/law applies).
- **Home** — Applies per occurrence for property claims; separate wind/hail deductibles may apply in some regions.
- **Life** — No deductible; benefits are paid per the face amount minus any outstanding loans on permanent policies (N/A for pure term).

## Subrogation

If we pay you for a loss caused by a third party, we may seek reimbursement from that party. You may need to cooperate and not release the responsible party without our agreement.

## Fraud

False or inflated claims may void coverage and lead to denial and referral to authorities.
