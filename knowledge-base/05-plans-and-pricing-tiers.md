# Plans and Coverage Levels

ShieldBase groups personal lines into **three convenience tiers** for comparison. Actual premiums use rating factors (location, limits, deductibles, credit where allowed, driving history, home characteristics, age/health for life, etc.).

## Auto — ShieldDrive tiers

| Tier | Liability limits | Physical damage | Typical profile |
|------|------------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Basic** | State minimum BI/PD | Collision/comprehensive optional; higher deductibles common | Budget-focused, owned-outright older vehicles |
| **Standard** | Elevated split limits or combined single limit mid-range | Collision/comprehensive with moderate deductibles | Everyday drivers, financed vehicles |
| **Comprehensive** | Higher liability plus recommended UM/UIM where available | Lower deductibles, rental/roadside endorsements often bundled | Newer vehicles, long commutes, max peace of mind |

## Home — ShieldHome tiers

| Tier | Dwelling / property | Deductible | Notes |
|------|----------------------|------------|--------|
| **Basic** | Lower replacement-cost-style limits, named-peril options where sold | Higher (e.g., $2,500+) | Smaller homes, tight budgets |
| **Standard** | Broad form / HO-3-style common | Moderate ($1,000–$2,500) | Typical family home |
| **Comprehensive** | Higher limits, scheduled personal property for valuables | Often lower wind/hail or all-peril deductible options | High-value homes, more endorsements |

## Life — ShieldTerm tiers

| Tier | Term length | Benefit focus |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| **Basic** | Shorter term (e.g., 10 years) | Income replacement for near-term obligations |
| **Standard** | Mid term (e.g., 20 years) | Mortgage and education funding |
| **Comprehensive** | Longer term (e.g., 30 years) plus riders | Maximum duration of protection while dependents rely on income |

Tiers are **labels for marketing clarity**; your quote PDF lists exact limits, deductibles, and premiums.
