# Underwriting, Rating, and Discounts

This document describes fictional but realistic ShieldBase underwriting and rating practices used for educational and chatbot simulation purposes.

## Rating factors by product

## Auto

Common rating inputs include:

- Garaging ZIP and territory characteristics
- Driver age, experience, and prior insurance history
- Vehicle year/make/model/trim and safety features
- Annual mileage and primary use (commute, pleasure, mixed)
- Prior violations/at-fault accidents within lookback period
- Coverage limits, deductibles, and selected endorsements

Illustrative note: in comparable scenarios, increasing collision deductible from `$500` to `$1,000` may reduce that coverage component while increasing out-of-pocket exposure after a claim.

## Home

Common rating inputs include:

- Construction type and age of home
- Roof age/material and mitigation features
- Fire protection class and distance to hydrant/station
- Prior property claims history
- Occupancy type (owner-occupied, seasonal, rental where eligible)
- Coverage A amount and deductible structure

## Life

Common rating inputs include:

- Age and selected term length
- Tobacco status and health class
- Medical and prescription history (where permitted)
- Occupation and avocation risk profile
- Requested face amount and rider elections

## Fictional underwriting outcomes

Applications are generally assigned one of these outcomes:

- **Preferred Plus** — Lowest modeled risk profile.
- **Preferred** — Strong risk profile with minor offsets.
- **Standard** — Typical risk profile for broad market eligibility.
- **Table-rated** — Higher risk class with adjusted premium.
- **Postpone/Decline** — Not currently eligible under guidelines.

Underwriting decisions may require follow-up documents and are subject to state/federal anti-discrimination rules and approved filing guidelines.

## Discount catalog (illustrative)

### Auto discounts

- Multi-vehicle
- Safe driver / violation-free period
- Telematics participation (where offered)
- Good student
- Defensive driving course completion
- Paid-in-full and paperless billing

### Home discounts

- Multi-policy bundle
- Protective devices (central alarm, leak sensors, smart shutoff)
- New purchase / new roof (eligibility windows apply)
- Claims-free history period

### Life discounts

- Non-tobacco rates
- Annual payment mode discount
- Preferred health classes

## Surcharges and ineligible combinations

Possible premium increases or restrictions can come from:

- Repeated at-fault losses in short periods
- Material misrepresentation discovered during underwriting
- Unacceptable use (for example, undisclosed commercial auto usage)
- Lapsed prior coverage within lookback windows

Some combinations may be unavailable (example: specific high-risk vehicles with lowest deductible options in selected territories).

## Renewal review cycle

At renewal, ShieldBase may adjust rates or eligibility based on:

- Updated filed rates and loss trends
- New claims or violation activity
- Coverage changes requested by policyholder
- Property condition updates from inspections (home)

Any adverse action follows notice requirements and applicable law.
